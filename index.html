About Me

Bachelor of Science graduate with a growing interest in the technology industry and web development.

During my undergraduate studies, I had the opportunity to take an Introduction to Coding elective, in which I learned how to make a game, as well as core units in which I learned how to use R to create data visualisations.

As a requirement of the workshop, I was able to create a website of great interest to me, and I enjoyed the creative aspects involved as well as finding solutions to fixing my code.
