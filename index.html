About Me

About Me

Bachelor of Science graduate with a growing interest in the technology industry and web development.

During my undergraduate studies, I had the opportunity to take an Introduction to Coding elective, in which I learned how to make a game, as well as core units in which I learned how to use R to create data visualisations.

Enrolling in a three-week SheCodes Basic Workshop also provided insight into front-end development and development tools. As a requirement of the workshop, I was able to create a website of great interest to me, and I enjoyed the creative aspects involved as well as finding solutions to fixing my code. Overall, I appreciate development's learning process, technical knowledge, and creativity.

Currently, I am planning to complete a 12-week Software Development Bootcamp by nology.
